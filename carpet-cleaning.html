FAQ

How often should I schedule carpet cleaning for my commercial space?

The frequency of carpet cleaning depends on factors such as foot traffic, type of business, and environmental conditions. High-traffic areas may require cleaning every 1 - 3 months, while lower-traffic areas can be cleaned every 6 months.

Will carpet cleaning disrupt my business operations?

We understand the importance of minimizing disruption to your business. Our team can work around your schedule to perform carpet cleaning during off-hours or weekends to ensure minimal disruption to your operations.

Can you remove tough stains and odors from our carpets?

Yes, our commercial carpet cleaning services are equipped to tackle tough stains and odors. We use specialized cleaning solutions and equipment to effectively remove stains and eliminate odors, leaving your carpets clean and fresh.

Is your carpet cleaning process environmentally friendly?

Yes, we prioritize environmentally friendly cleaning methods and use eco-friendly products whenever possible. Our cleaning solutions are safe for the environment and for your employees and customers.

Carpet Cleaning
