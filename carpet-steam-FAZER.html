FAQ

How often should I have my carpets steam cleaned?

We recommend steam cleaning your carpets at least once a year to maintain their appearance and hygiene. For homes with pets, children, or high foot traffic, every 6 months is advisable.

Is steam cleaning safe for all types of carpets?

Steam cleaning is safe for most carpet types. However, some delicate materials may require a specific approach. We assess each situation to ensure the safest and most effective cleaning method is used.

How long will it take for my carpets to dry after steam cleaning?

Carpets typically take 4 to 6 hours to dry completely after steam cleaning, depending on ventilation and air humidity. We recommend keeping windows open to facilitate faster drying.

Can steam cleaning remove all stains?

Steam cleaning can remove most stains, but results can vary based on the age and type of the stain. We pre-treat stains to enhance the effectiveness of the cleaning process.

Carpet Steam Cleaning

Carpet Steam Cleaning utilizes high-temperature steam to deep clean carpets, effectively removing dirt, stains, and allergens. This method penetrates deep into carpet fibers, dissolving dirt and grime without the use of harsh chemicals. It's ideal for homes and offices, helping to restore the appearance and texture of your carpets. Regular steam cleaning can also extend the life of your carpets by preventing the buildup of abrasive particles that wear down carpet fibers. It’s an eco-friendly solution that enhances indoor air quality and provides a healthier environment.

Benefits of Carpet Steam Cleaning

  • Removes deep-seated dirt and stains
  • Extends the life of carpets
  • Improves indoor air quality by removing allergens
  • Eco-friendly; minimal use of chemicals
  • Restores carpet texture and appearance
