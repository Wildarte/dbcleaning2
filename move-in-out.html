FAQ

What specific areas do you focus on for move-in/out cleaning?

Our move-in/out cleaning service focuses on preparing a property for the next occupant. This includes deep cleaning of all rooms, appliances, cabinets, drawers, and fixtures to ensure that every surface is spotless.

How does move-in/out cleaning differ from deep cleaning?

While both services are thorough, move-in/out cleaning is tailored to transitioning homes between occupants, ensuring that every nook and cranny is addressed so the space is pristine for new occupants, including inside of cabinets and drawers.

Do you provide any documentation to confirm the cleaning was completed?

Yes, we can provide photographic documentation detailing the areas that have been cleaned to confirm the completion of our services, if requested.

Is it necessary to have utilities turned on for move-in/out cleaning?

Yes, having utilities like electricity and water is essential to perform a thorough cleaning, especially for tasks like steam cleaning carpets and powering vacuums.

Move In/Out

Transition with confidence using our Move In/Out Cleaning service. It mirrors the comprehensive nature of our Deep Cleaning, but is fine-tuned for an empty home. With no personal items in the way, we’re able to conduct an even more in-depth clean, attending to every nook within cabinets and closets. As you embark on a new beginning or conclude a familiar chapter, let us handle the details of a pristine space, lessening the burden of moving and allowing you to focus on what’s next.

Benefits of Move In/Out

  • Ensures a fresh start for new occupants or a smooth transition for those moving out.
  • Thoroughly cleans and sanitizes all areas of the property, including hidden spaces.
  • Helps fulfill lease or rental agreement requirements for cleanliness.
  • Reduces stress and saves time for individuals during the moving process.
  • Enhances the appeal of the property for potential buyers or renters.
