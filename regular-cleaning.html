FAQ

What does regular cleaning include?

Regular cleaning covers the basics that keep your home looking neat and tidy, including dusting, vacuuming, mopping, cleaning the bathrooms and kitchen, and making beds. It's designed to maintain a level of cleanliness around the house.

How frequently should I book a regular cleaning service?

The frequency depends on your lifestyle, family size, and home usage. Most customers prefer weekly or bi-weekly cleanings, but we also offer monthly services for less trafficked homes.

Can I customize the tasks included in my regular cleaning plan?

Absolutely! We are flexible with our service and happy to customize our regular cleaning to suit your specific needs and priorities.

Will the same cleaner come each time?

We strive to send the same cleaner for each visit so that they become familiar with your preferences and specific needs. However, in cases of unpredictable situations, a similarly qualified cleaner will take over without compromising the quality of service.

Regular Cleaning

This is a maintenance service and it is available after we perform our Initial Deep Cleaning. For this service, we have a set flat rate per cleaning frequency while your contract is valid. We have 3 frequency options available to make sure our service fits your property and schedule needs. As a maintenance service, this cleaning does not include the inside of your appliances, windows, or inside of your cabinets, however, those can be included in regular service as an add-on service for an adjusted rate.

Benefits of Regular Cleaning

  • Maintains cleanliness and hygiene on a consistent basis.
  • Reduces the buildup of dirt and dust, preventing the need for deep cleaning as frequently.
  • Creates a comfortable and inviting environment for occupants.
  • Supports a healthier indoor environment by minimizing allergens and germs.
  • Customizable to fit the specific needs and preferences of clients.

What’s included in your Regular Cleaning

Please note that this cleaning checklist can be adapted to better suit your needs.

All Rooms

  • Cobwebs removed
  • Dust top of door frames and doorknobs
  • All light fixtures and fan blades dusted
  • Dust windows blinds, sills and frames
  • Dust baseboards, switch plates and outlet cover
  • Dusting of wall hangings
  • Dust decorative accessories (pictures, collections, etc.)
  • Glass and mirrors cleaned and sparkled
  • Furniture dusted and polished on top and sides where accessible
  • Vacuum carpets and area rugs
  • Clean underneath area rugs (where possible)
  • Hard floors swept and damp mopped
  • Sweep Pet Food Area
  • Empty wastebaskets and reline only

Bedrooms

  • Vacuum under bed (if accessible)
  • Lamps and lamp shades dusted
  • All Beds made only
  • Bed linens changed (1 bed included only), if provided
  • Trash emptied

Family, Living and Dining Rooms

  • Dust Fireplace Mantel Only
  • Vacuum under furniture
  • Vacuum hard surfaces under cushions
  • Lamp and lampshades dusted
  • Dining room table dusted/cleaned

Utility Rooms

  • Exterior of washer and dryer cleaned
  • Clean and sanitize utility sink

Hallways, Entries and Foyers

  • Entry door window cleaned

Additional Requests

  • Inside Refrigerator Cleaning
  • Inside Oven Cleaning
  • Carpet Cleaning
  • Upholstery Cleaning
